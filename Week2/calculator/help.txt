Welcome to the Subject Calculator!
This application allows you to add subjects along with their pass rates.
You can then enter student marks for each subject and check if they pass.
To add a subject, enter the subject name and pass rate in the provided entry fields,\n
then click the 'Add Subject' button. To delete a subject, select it from the list and click 'Delete Subject'.\n\n
Once subjects are added, you can enter student marks by clicking the 'Enter Student Marks' button,\n
and the application will calculate whether the student passed each subject.\n\n
